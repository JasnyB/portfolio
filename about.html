Senior Customer Sales Representative – Hertz Bilutleie

At Hertz, I am responsible for customer service, fleet planning and coordination, car rentals, and sales of related products and services. I handle daily customer inquiries via phone, email, and in person.

Part of my role involves assisting customers with technical challenges, including the use of our Self-Service Kiosk. I communicate fluently in Norwegian, English, and German, adapting my communication style to suit different customer needs.

My workday consists of a combination of car rentals, cleaning and preparing vehicles, fleet planning, and vehicle check-in. Upon return, I carry out damage inspections and record findings in our systems.

Every fourth weekend, I have the main responsibility for the entire station, which involves managing daily operations and handling any urgent situations. This has given me valuable experience in team leadership, task delegation, and decision-making under pressure. I often act as a mentor for new employees and serve as a natural point of contact for colleagues and customers when challenges or questions arise.
